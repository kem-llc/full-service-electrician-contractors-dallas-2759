Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electricians Since 1998

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services to the Dallas community, with a commitment to integrity, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction.

Full Service Electrician Contractors was founded in 1998 by master electrician John Martinez. Starting with a single truck and a vision to raise the bar for electrical service in Dallas, John built a reputation for honest work and fair pricing. Over the past 25+ years, we've grown into a team of 20+ licensed electricians, serving thousands of residential and commercial clients across the Dallas metro area.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us a perfect 5.0-star rating and recognition as one of Dallas's top electrical contractors. We invest in ongoing training, cutting-edge tools, and strict safety protocols to ensure every job meets the highest standards. Whether it's a simple repair or a complex new construction project, we treat every home and business like our own.
