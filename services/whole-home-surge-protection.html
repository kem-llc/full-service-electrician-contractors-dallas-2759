Whole-Home Surge Protection

Protect your electronics and appliances from power surges.

Schedule this service

Overview

Lightning strikes, grid switching, and large appliances can cause damaging power surges. Our whole-home surge protectors are installed at your main panel to safeguard all connected devices. We offer both Type 1 and Type 2 surge protection devices (SPDs) from leading manufacturers.

What's included

  • Protects expensive electronics like TVs, computers, and appliances
  • Prevents data loss and equipment damage
  • Reduces fire risk from surge-related overheating
  • Peace of mind with lifetime warranty on select units

How it works

1

Site Evaluation

We inspect your panel and determine the best surge protection solution for your home.

2

Installation

We install the SPD at the main panel, properly grounding and bonding the system.

3

Testing & Verification

We test the unit to ensure it's functioning correctly and provide documentation.
