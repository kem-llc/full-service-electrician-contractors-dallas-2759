Electrical Panel Upgrades

Upgrade your electrical panel for safety, capacity, and code compliance.

Schedule this service

Overview

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's electrical system. If you have flickering lights, tripping breakers, or need to add new appliances, it may be time for an upgrade. Our licensed electricians install top-brand panels (Square D, Siemens, Eaton) with proper load calculations and permits.

What's included

  • Increased electrical capacity for modern appliances
  • Enhanced safety with arc-fault and ground-fault protection
  • Reduced risk of electrical fires and code violations
  • Higher home resale value

How it works

1

Inspection & Load Calculation

We assess your current panel, measure load, and identify any safety issues.

2

Permit & Material Procurement

We handle all city permits and source high-quality materials.

3

Installation & Testing

Our team installs the new panel, connects circuits, and tests everything for safe operation.
