Electrical Panel Upgrades

Upgrade your electrical panel for increased safety, capacity, and modern appliance compatibility.

Schedule this service

Overview

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's electrical system. If it's outdated or overloaded, you risk flickering lights, tripped breakers, and even fire. We replace old fuse boxes and panels with modern, safe units that handle today's power demands. Our licensed electricians ensure a smooth upgrade that meets all code requirements.

What's included

  • Eliminate tripped breakers and flickering lights
  • Support for modern appliances and electronics
  • Increased home safety and insurance compliance
  • Adds value to your property

How it works

1

Inspection & Assessment

We evaluate your current panel, load requirements, and wiring to recommend the right upgrade.

2

Permit & Planning

We handle all necessary permits and plan the installation to minimize downtime.

3

Professional Installation

Our electricians install the new panel, test all circuits, and ensure everything operates safely.

Common questions

How do I know if I need a panel upgrade?

Common signs: frequent breaker trips, buzzing sounds, old fuse box, or if you're adding major appliances like an EV charger or AC unit.

How long does a panel upgrade take?

Most residential upgrades take 4-8 hours. We'll provide a specific timeline during your estimate.
