Whole-Home Rewiring

Complete rewiring for older homes to ensure safety, reliability, and modern electrical capacity.

Schedule this service

Overview

Homes built before 1980 often have outdated wiring that can't handle today's electrical loads and may pose fire hazards. Our whole-home rewiring service replaces all old wiring, outlets, and switches with modern, code-compliant materials. We work carefully to minimize wall damage and restore your home to pristine condition.

What's included

  • Eliminates fire hazards from old or damaged wiring
  • Supports modern electronics and appliances
  • Improves energy efficiency
  • Peace of mind with a fully updated system

How it works

1

Full Home Inspection

We inspect all wiring, outlets, and the panel to identify issues and plan the rewiring.

2

Phased Installation

We rewire room by room to minimize disruption, running new cables and installing modern outlets and switches.

3

Final Testing & Cleanup

All circuits are tested for safety and performance. We patch any access holes and leave your home clean.

Common questions

How long does a whole-home rewiring take?

Typically 3-7 days depending on home size. We'll provide a detailed timeline upfront.

Do I need to move out during rewiring?

Not necessarily. We work in sections so you can stay in unaffected areas. We'll discuss your situation.
